"Save Money and Reduce Food Waste"

Fresh Save is a system designed to help you save money and reduce food waste with products that keep food fresh, a recipe generator, a free food marketplace, and a barcode scanning feature that provides information about compatibility and proper storage, all promoting a sustainable and equitable food system.

    • Features

    • SCAN FOOD ITEMS

      • The barcode scanning feature enables users to quickly and easily access crucial information about a food item such as the ingredients, nutritional value, and allergens. By simply scanning the barcode with their phone camera, users can make informed decisions about what they eat and avoid any potential health risks.

    • FOOD ITEMS INVENTORY

      • The "View Inventory" feature allows users to easily keep track of their stored or scanned food items and their respective use by dates. With a quick glance, users can see what food products they have on hand and plan their meals accordingly, reducing food waste and saving money.

    • FREE FOOD COMMUNITIES

      • The free food community feature allows users to connect with others in their community who are either in need of or have excess food items that are close to their expiry dates. Users can either donate or receive food items, and also have the option to sell large food items for a discounted price. This feature promotes food sharing and reduces food waste while also supporting those in need.

    FOOD CATEGORIZATION

    The food categorization feature allows users to quickly sort scanned food items into fresh, canned or other categories, helping them keep track of their groceries and better plan meals. This feature is designed to save time and help users stay organized when it comes to managing their food inventory.

    The day warning feature reminds users of the remaining shelf life of scanned food items and provides an alert when the item is about to expire. This helps users to be aware of the freshness of their food and avoid wasting it by using it before it spoils.

    RECIPE GENERATION

    The "Recipe Generator" feature allows users to quickly and easily generate meal ideas based on the ingredients they have available in their inventory. By simply selecting the ingredients they have on hand or inputting custom items, the app will generate a list of recipes that can be made with those ingredients. Users can filter the results by cuisine type, dietary restrictions, and cooking time to find the perfect recipe for their needs. This feature helps users reduce food waste by encouraging them to use up ingredients they already have instead of letting them go bad.

    RECEIVE OR DONATE

    The "Donate Food" feature enables users to give back to their communities by donating food items to those in need. Users can select the food items they want to donate, specify the quantity, and indicate their location. Once the donation is confirmed, the recipient will receive a notification with the location of the donor and the item's expiry date to ensure transparency. This feature encourages users to reduce food waste and promote social responsibility by helping those who may not have access to fresh food items.

    About Us

    We are the ultimate food waste reduction app designed to help you manage your food inventory, reduce food waste, and save money! Our app provides users with a range of features to help them effectively manage their food items. Easily categorize your food items into fresh, canned, or other categories, ensuring that you always know what you have and what you need to use up before it expires. Our app also provides a helpful feature that alerts you to how many days you have left before an item expires, giving you plenty of time to use it up or find a recipe that includes it.

    Have you ever found yourself staring at a food item in your pantry or fridge, wondering what to make with it? Our app has got you covered with our barcode scanner feature, which allows you to scan a food item and instantly receive important information about it, including its nutritional value, ingredients, and allergens. But that's not all! Fresh Save also includes a recipe generator feature that suggests recipes based on the items in your inventory, making meal planning and grocery shopping a breeze. And if you have a food item that you won't be able to use up in time, you can either donate it to other users who may need it or sell it for a discounted price.
